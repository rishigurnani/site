About Me

This site is a curated collection of links and projects on Machine Learning and Materials Science. I currently serve as the Director of Software Engineering & Algorithms at Matmerize and engage in innovative materials informatics research.

My work focuses on harnessing deep learning and computational methods to discover novel materials for energy storage and beyond.
